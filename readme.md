# EasyRim

[简体中文](readme.zh-CN.md)

EasyRim is a deliberately overpowered RimWorld mod focused on stronger colonists, faster production, and better world-map mobility.

> **Warning:** EasyRim is intentionally unbalanced and will significantly reduce the game's difficulty.

## Core enhancement systems

- **Transcendence:** cognitive, physical, biological, environmental, and psychic upgrades, plus adaptive armor and a permanent nanite battle-morph toolset.
- **Nano emergency reconstruction:** the biological transcendence branch can prevent death and reconstruct a critically wounded pawn after a delay.
- **Brave Soul:** staged vitality and combat bonuses with powerful regeneration and melee pressure.
- **Blood Essence Resonance:** bonuses and penalties change dynamically with hemogen reserves across apex, flow, balance, ebb, and famine stages.
- **Pioneer Spirit:** broad improvements to labor, hauling, shooting, recovery, and survival.
- **Adaptive nexus spine:** a craftable artificial spine that distributes existing enhancement branches and dynamically responds to health, combat, genes, and equipped gear.

## Abilities

- **Crimson Steel Infusion:** briefly reduces incoming damage.
- **Forbidden Restoration:** rapidly heals serious injuries and restores blood.
- **Flesh Sculptor:** turns a corpse into an uncontrolled fleshbeast.
- **Flesh Dissolution:** kills a living target with a devastating psychic attack.
- **Universal Bloodfeed:** drains any valid living, bleeding target for increased hemogen gain.
- **Eternal Revival:** resurrects without a resource cost.
- **Nano Disassembly Transit:** provides rapid movement, including world-map destinations.
- **Overmind Drops:** calls several single-use drop pods.
- **Work Guidance:** temporarily adds up to 10 effective skill levels and triples learning speed for the target pawn.
- **Colony Mobilization:** grants map-wide mood and work-speed bonuses to eligible colonists.

## Buildings and production

- **Auto Drill:** mines deep resources without an operator, runs only with available sunlight, shuts down when deposits are exhausted, and can be reinstalled elsewhere.
- **Buildable Life Support Unit:** pressurizes and heats rooms while producing substantial power.
- **Batch components:** craft industrial and advanced components in larger batches.
- **Neutroamine:** synthesize it from chemfuel, herbal medicine, and psychoid leaves.
- **Textiles:** craft synthread and hyperweave.

## Accelerated systems and balance changes

- Greatly shortened medic, bioregeneration, age-reversal, and pleasure biosculpter cycles.
- Faster, fixed production cadence for the infinite chemreactor.
- Faster wastepack atomization with larger internal storage.
- Greatly increased geothermal-generator and ship-reactor output.
- Deep-resource detection for player-controlled tunneler mechs.

Most character power is implemented through Hediffs and Abilities. `TraitDef` currently contains no gameplay entries.
